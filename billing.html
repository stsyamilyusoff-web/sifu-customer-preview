Parent billing · nine screens, one skin, fixed bugs
Cluster C3 of the customer-app redesign, expanded. Every billing and payment screen, each before and after, on the parent design language already in the app's code. Sharpens the three screens the redesign already touched (list, Pay-All, commitment fee) and adds the six detail and payment screens, fixing the brand typo, the contradictory due date, the misleading post-charge error, and the raw backend strings. For Hafiz · 26 June 2026.
Three billing screens were already redesigned (hero cards, group-by-request, receipt feed, Pay-All) and are reused here, sharpened. The other six (the invoice and receipt details, the commitment-fee detail, the refund detail, the gateway step, and the success screen) are still rough or still on the old look. This brings all nine onto one language and fixes the real bugs found in code: the PDF and support email read "Siftutor" (missing a "u"), the PDF due date is both wrong and self-contradictory, a successful charge can still show "Payment successful but network error occurred", and raw backend error strings reach the parent. Each screen below is its own before/after pair.

What this is, and is not (Step 2.5 reassess)
Applied: a global "pay everything" on the list; one consistent total and per-line due state on Pay-All; the commitment fee explained (it credits the first invoice) with a clear oldest-first block; a due date on the invoice detail, decluttered of restated personal fields; the paid receipt and refund framed correctly (money paid, money returned); the commitment-fee receipt moved off the old flat layout onto the hero-card language; the gateway step given context and a handoff cue; the success screen given the real amount and a calm pending fallback. Across all nine: the consistency floor (tokens, one brand blue, "Sifututor" casing everywhere, neutral plain copy, real loading and error states).
Kept on purpose: the hero cards, group-by-request cards, receipt feed, and Pay-All bundle are already redesigned and good, so they are sharpened, not replaced. Price stays as today (no commission breakdown). No field is cut for matching or accounting, only the restated personal fields are dropped from the bill view, where the request already carries them.
Real bugs fixed (verified in code)
Brand typo: the invoice and receipt PDFs and the support email read "Siftutor" and hello@siftutor.my (missing the "u"), in CommitmentFeePending, CommitmentFeeDetail, and the shared invoice/receipt body. Every PDF a parent forwards shows the wrong brand. Fix to "Sifututor" and hello@sifututor.my.
Wrong, self-contradictory due date: the invoice PDF header prints "Due (created date + 6 days)" while the fine print below says "Payment is due within 3 working days". Neither matches the billing rule (cycle date + 7). The AFTER shows one due date, derived from the rule, on screen and in the PDF.
Misleading post-charge message: when the gateway succeeds but the backend confirmation times out or returns 4xx/5xx, the parent sees "Payment successful but network error occurred" (in SharedInvoiceDetail, PayAllSummary, and CommitmentFeePending). The AFTER replaces it with a calm "your payment is safe, confirmation is on the way" line plus a transaction reference.
Raw backend strings shown to the parent: failed verify paths show "104: (raw error)" and similar from the response body straight in a popup. The AFTER maps these to plain, neutral messages with a reference, and keeps the raw detail in telemetry only.
