The consistency floor (the standard each screen must meet)
  • Design tokens, not hard-coded colors or sizes (one source of truth).
  • One brand blue and one radius scale across all screens.
  • Brand spelled Sifututor everywhere (no "SifuTutor", no "Siftutor").
  • Neutral, plain copy. No em-dashes.
  • One date format app-wide (DD-MMM-YYYY).
  • Real loading, empty, and error states. No blank screens, no raw error text.
  • Consistent headers, buttons, and spacing between sibling screens.
