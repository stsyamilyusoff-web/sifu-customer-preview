Parent request flow · all eight screens, one skin, fewer taps
C1 of the customer-app redesign. Every screen of the request and matching funnel, each as its own before and after, on the parent design language already in the app's code. Three real defects are shown in the BEFORE and fixed in the AFTER. For Hafiz · 26 June 2026.
The funnel is honest: every field feeds the price, the matching, the schedule, or the address, so this is a tap cut, not a field cut. Three real defects are addressed here: (1) the five steps keep state only in memory, so a backgrounded app wipes a half-filled request; (2) the detail screen hard-codes "Matching in progress, within 24 hours" no matter the real status, which can contradict the applied-tutor count on the same screen; (3) switching mode silently clears the chosen subject with no hint. The AFTER applies the consistency floor: the tokens, brand blue, "Sifututor" cased right, neutral copy, real loading and empty states, a draft that survives a restart, a status that reflects reality, and smart defaults that name their source.

The defect. Every funnel field lives in NewTutorRequestFormContext, and the defaults are set in a mount-time effect. Nothing is written to storage. iOS or Android reclaiming the app mid-request wipes the lot, the parent reopens to a blank form.

The fix. Persist the in-flight request (the same shape the context already holds) to local storage on each step, clear it on submit. On return, the list shows a "Continue your draft" banner and the funnel can rehydrate.

Why it matters. The drop-off here is invisible today: a parent who got two taps from submitting and lost it may simply not come back. This is pure client-side, zero backend.

Guard: a draft is clearly labelled and easy to discard, never a silent pre-fill the parent cannot see or clear.

What the reassess changed (Step 2.5, founder-approved)

The three real defects, verified in code
1 · A backgrounded app wipes a half-filled request. The funnel state lives only in NewTutorRequestFormContext (in-memory), with defaults set in a mount-time effect and nothing written to storage. Fix: persist the draft per step, offer "Continue your draft" on the list, clear on submit. Zero backend.
2 · The detail status is hard-coded. RequestDetails always renders "Matching in progress · SifuTutor will assign your best-fit tutor within 24 hours", directly under the live "N Tutors Applied" count, so the two can contradict each other and the line stays "in progress" even once matched. Fix: drive the status from the real state, hide the internal tally from parents, case "Sifututor" correctly.
3 · A mode switch silently clears the subject. RequestNow's handleModeSelect resets subject and level on every mode tap with no hint. Fix: keep the selection across a mode change, and confirm before clearing anything that would not carry over.
